2014–2020: The AI Boom

This was the most exciting time in AI history. Machines became smarter than ever. They could recognize faces, understand speech, translate languages, and drive cars.

Deep Learning Revolution

New machine learning models were created that worked like a human brain. These models are called neural networks. They made AI extremely accurate in prediction and recognition.

Achievements of This Era

Self-driving cars
Robots that walk and run
Real-time language translation
Face unlock in smartphones

AI in Reality

Hospitals used AI to detect diseases. Factories used robots for work. Apps like YouTube, Netflix, Instagram used AI to recommend videos and posts.

Why It Matters

The world started trusting AI. It moved from small devices to industries, medicine, transport, and entertainment.
