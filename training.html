1. Data Input in AI

AI systems are trained using large collections of data known as datasets. These datasets may include images, speech recordings, videos, numbers, or written text. The more data AI receives, the more accurately it learns patterns and improves its decision-making.

For example:
✅ AI learns to recognize a cat by studying thousands of cat pictures.
✅ Voice assistants like Alexa or Google learn from recorded speech.
✅ ChatGPT learns from billions of sentences written by humans.

During data input, AI’s job is to collect information, read it, and store it in a structured form. It does not become intelligent immediately — the journey begins only after the data is fed into the system.

Without data, AI cannot learn anything. That is why data input is the first and most important step of building intelligent machines.
