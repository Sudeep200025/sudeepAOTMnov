2000–2010: AI Enters Everyday Life

Between 2000 and 2010, AI moved from laboratories into the real world. Technology became faster, cheaper, and available to everyone. People used AI daily without even realizing it.

Where AI Started Appearing

✅ Face detection in cameras
✅ Auto-suggestions in search engines
✅ Recommended videos & music
✅ Early voice assistants

AI in Gaming

Games like chess, racing, and strategy games used AI to play against humans. This was the first time normal people saw how computers could make decisions on their own.

Smarter Phones

Smartphones learned user patterns — typing prediction, photo filters, and face unlock were all possible because of AI-powered algorithms.

Impact of This Decade

AI became a part of daily life. It helped computers understand language, speech, and images. This decade prepared the world for self-driving cars, robots, and modern AI systems.
